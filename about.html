RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM

It is a type of decision support system designed to discover user preferences, and to study them in order to anticipate their needs. They provide recommendations to customers as per their taste within a given domain.A recommendation system is an artificial intelligence or AI algorithm, usually associated with machine learning, that uses Big Data to suggest or recommend additional products to consumers. These can be based on various criteria, including past purchases, search history, demographic information, and other factors.
